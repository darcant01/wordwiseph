About Word Sprint

Word Sprint is a fast-paced English word game that challenges Filipino kids to recognize, read and respond to English words as quickly as possible. In Word Sprint, players race against the clock to identify and select correct English words based on definitions, categories or visual clues. This game builds reading speed, word recognition and vocabulary — three skills that directly affect a child's performance in all school subjects.

Reading speed is a critical skill that many Filipino students struggle with. Slow reading affects comprehension across all subjects — from English class to Math word problems. Word Sprint trains the brain to recognize whole words instantly rather than sounding out each letter, which is the key to achieving fluent reading speed. Regular Word Sprint practice has been shown to measurably improve reading speed within just a few weeks.

Difficulty Levels and Progression

Word Sprint features Easy, Medium and Hard difficulty levels that progressively increase in speed and vocabulary complexity. Easy level uses high-frequency words appropriate for early grade learners. Medium introduces Grade 3 to Grade 4 vocabulary at a faster pace. Hard challenges Grade 5 and Grade 6 students with academic vocabulary and very tight time limits. Players can track their personal best scores and compete on the WordWise PH leaderboard.

For Filipino Students and Teachers

Word Sprint is recommended as a daily warm-up activity for Filipino students in Grades 2 through 6. It is available on paid plans starting at ₱10 per day via GCash, with no automatic billing or renewal. Play at wordwiseph.com on any mobile, tablet or desktop browser — no download needed.
