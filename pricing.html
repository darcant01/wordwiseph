What's the difference between Basic and Premium? ▾
Basic covers 1 child account with all games, Kids Corner, and full Letter Tracer access. Premium adds up to 5 child accounts, an advanced parent dashboard, and priority support.

Yes! The Free plan lets you play Grammar Quiz (Easy mode) with 4 rounds at no cost. You can also try the Trial plan for just ₱10/day to test everything before committing to a monthly plan.
