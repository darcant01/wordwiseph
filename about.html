How it started

The WordWise PH story

💡
The idea
Darwin noticed that English learning apps for kids were either too expensive or just not engaging enough to keep kids coming back.
🛠️
Building from scratch
Every game, every question, every animation — built by hand. No team, no funding, just code and a clear vision of what kids actually enjoy.
🎮
7 Brainy Games launched
Grammar Quiz, Spell It Right, Shark Typing, Odd One Out, Word Scramble, Word Chain, and Word Sprint — all live and free to try.
🌟
Kids Corner added
16 games for younger learners — Letter Tracer with accuracy checking, Animal Games with real sounds, Color Games, and more.
🚀
Still growing
New games, features, and better tools — driven by feedback from real families. The mission stays the same.
