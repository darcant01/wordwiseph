Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

WordWise PH ("we," "our") is an English learning platform for children. We take privacy seriously — especially children's privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

  • Account information: full name, username, password, and account type (kid/adult) when you register.
  • Game data: scores, game rounds played, and progress — used for leaderboards and your score history.
  • Payment information: your GCash reference number when you subscribe. We never see or store your GCash PIN, balance, or full account details.
💡 We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, exact location, photos, or contacts. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that profile children.

2. How We Use It

  • To create and manage your account and subscription
  • To save scores and show leaderboards (only your username is visible to others)
  • To verify payments and activate plans
  • To respond to support requests

3. What We Never Do

  • ❌ We never sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third parties
  • ❌ We never show ads to children
  • ❌ We never contact children directly

4. Children's Privacy

WordWise PH is designed for children to use with parental involvement. We recommend that a parent or guardian creates the account and manages the subscription. If you believe a child has provided information without parental consent, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

5. Data Storage & Security

Account data is stored securely in Google's cloud infrastructure (Google Sheets/Apps Script). Passwords are used only to log you in. Access to user data is limited to the site administrator.

6. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing darcant01@gmail.com or messaging @wordwiseph_support_bot. We will delete it within 7 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of WordWise PH means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions? Email darcant01@gmail.com or message our support bot on Telegram: @wordwiseph_support_bot.
